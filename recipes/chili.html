Chili



Description

This chili will warm you up on a chilly afternoon, and keep you going all day.

I left out quantities as this recipe is very verstile and forgiving.

Ingredients

  • olive oil
  • chli spices
  • onions
  • garlic
  • celery
  • beef
  • canned tomaotes
  • black and kidney beans
  • corn
  • tomato juice

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a cast iron skillet (add enough to generously cover bottlom of skillet
  2. Add your chili spices to oil and wait for it start to smell good
  3. Add onions, garlic and celery and cook till soft and aromatic
  4. Now scrape them all into a slow cooker
  5. Return skillet to heat, add beef, and cook till brown
  6. Scrape beef into slow cooker
  7. Return skillet to heat, deglaze skillet, and add drippings to slow cooker
  8. To the slow cooker, add canned tomatoes, beans, corn, anything else you like in your chili
  9. Add tomato juice until all ingredients are covered
  10. Put lid on slow cooker, and set it to low, or high, dependingn on when you want to eat
  11. When it smells good, eat it over mashed potatoes (trust me)
