Pancakes



Description

These pancakes pair perfectly with lazy Saturday mornings

Ingredients

  • 1 1/4 cups flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • dash of cinnamon
  • Dash of salt

  • 1 beaten egg
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • dash of vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Mix wet ingredients in one bowl
  2. Mix dry ingredients in a different, larger bowl
  3. Make a well in the dry ingredients and pour wet ingredients into it
  4. By hand, mix ingredients together until just combined - small lumps okay
  5. Heat skillet over medium heat until droplets of water skittle across
  6. Pour 1/4 cup of batter on skillet
  7. When you see bubbles form, flip the pancake
  8. Cook pancake until center is dry
  9. Adjust heat as needed

Tip: you can add goodies - chocolate chips, banana slices, blueberries - while waiting for the bubbles to form in step 7
