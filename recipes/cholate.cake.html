Chocolate Cake



Description

This cake is simple to make, moist and vegan! Can't beat that

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup Vegetable oil
  • 2 cups cold water
  • 2 tablespoons vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon vanila

  • 3 cups flour
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1/2 cup cocoa
  • 2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • dash of cinnamon

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Grease a 9X13 pan, or two 8 inch round pans
  3. Mix wet ingredients in one bowl
  4. Mix dry ingredients in a different, larger bowl
  5. Make a well in the dry ingredients and pour wet ingredients into it
  6. By hand, mix ingredients together until just combined - small lumps okay
  7. Pour batter into pan / pans
  8. Bake until a knife comes out clean from the middle, about 40 minutes
